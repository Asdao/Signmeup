# User Guide

This guide explains how to interact with the three main modules of the **UnifierBBinary** suite.

---

## Aurora AI Assistant

Aurora is your proactive wellness partner.

### Key Features:
- **Dashboard**: View your "Safety Score," "Hydration Level," and "Emotional State" in real-time.
- **Chat**: Talk to Aurora for advice, reminders, or just a friendly conversation.
- **Vision Monitoring**: Aurora watches for:
    - **Falls**: Automatic alert if a fall is detected.
    - **Hydration**: Tracks when you drink water to ensure you stay healthy.
    - **Emotions**: Senses if you are happy, sad, or stressed.

### How to use:
1. Open [http://localhost:5173](http://localhost:5173).
2. Allow camera access.
3. Stay within the camera's view for monitoring.
4. Type or speak to interact with the AI.

---

## PathGuardian 2.0

PathGuardian provides independent mobility with a safety net.

### Key Features:
- **Large Control Buttons**: Designed for users with limited motor skills or visual impairments.
- **Voice Navigation**: Say your destination to start a route.
- **Caregiver Alerts**: If you wander off the planned path, your caregiver is notified immediately.

### How to use:
1. Open [http://localhost:8081](http://localhost:8081).
2. Select "Senior Mode" or "User Mode."
3. Click "Walk to National Museum" (Simulation) to see the system in action.
4. Voice commands can be triggered by tapping the microphone icon.

---

## SignMeUp Interpreter

SignMeUp bridges the gap for non-verbal communication.

### Key Features:
- **Custom Sign Training**: Teach the system your specific hand signs.
- **Instant Translation**: Real-time text output as you sign.
- **High Privacy**: Processes everything in the browser; no video is sent to the cloud.

### How to use:
1. Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000).
2. Go to the "Training" section to teach a new sign (e.g., hold up your hand for "Medicine").
3. Use the "Interpreter" mode to translate your signs in real-time during a conversation.

---

## Caregiver Integration

Caregivers can monitor status through the shared dashboard across modules.
- **Real-time Map**: Track location on the PathGuardian dashboard.
- **Health Summary**: View Aurora's wellness logs.
- **SOS Notifications**: Receive instant alerts for falls or deviations.
